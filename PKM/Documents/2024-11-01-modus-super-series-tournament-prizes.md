## Notes

- Group position at end of Mon–Wed/Thu–Fri league play determines Friday/Saturday finals entry
- Prize money paid via BACS within 17 days of session end
- FEU tax (Foreign Entertainers Unit) applied for non-UK residents; amount depends on FEU8 certification
