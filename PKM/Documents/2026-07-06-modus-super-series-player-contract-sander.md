# MODUS Super Series Player Contract

## Summary

Legally binding player contract between Sander van Ockenburg-Zwaan (player, self-employed professional) and MODUS Online Limited (Series 15 tournament operator). Signed 06-07-2026 by Jason Thame (Director, MOL).

**Key clauses:**
- Self-employed professional status; no employment relationship
- Exclusive participation in MODUS Super Series (no other live-streamed darts competitions without MOL approval)
- Dress code & sponsor logo placement compliance required
- Anti-doping, anti-match-fixing, and player conduct obligations
- IPR license granted to MOL for promotional materials and live/edited recordings

- FEU tax withholding for non-UK residents (Sander declared non-resident, NL address)
- Dispute resolution via Civil Mediation Council (CMC) before court
