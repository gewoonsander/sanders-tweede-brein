# Behandelplan Xanne-Lynn — Bureau Focus Afd. 11

Specialized youth mental health treatment plan (ggz) for Xanne-Lynn van Ockenburg-Zwaan, born August 27, 2012.

## Plan details

- **Issued:** July 6, 2026
- **Provider:** Bureau Focus, specialized youth mental health services
- **Organization contact:** Hortensiaalaan 1a, 6851 TB Huissen
- **Clinical focus:** Emotional regulation, anxiety management, behavioral support
- **Treatment approach:** System-based individual and family therapy

## Key elements from the plan

### Presenting situation

Xanne-Lynn lives at home with two older siblings. She shows a pattern of sudden emotional outbursts and behavioral disruptions due to anxiety and emotional dysregulation. She has peer relationships but experiences social difficulties when conflict arises.

### Treatment goals

The plan focuses on:
1. Strengthening emotional regulation and coping skills
2. Building self-understanding and managing emotions more mindfully
3. Improving quality of life through reduced emotional reactivity and increased behavioral flexibility
4. Supporting parents in understanding and validating emotions while setting appropriate limits

### Interventions

- System-based family therapy
- Individual therapy sessions
- Parent consultation and coaching

### Evaluation

Evaluation planned after three months; treatment continuation or adjustment to be reviewed based on progress.
