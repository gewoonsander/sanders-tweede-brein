# MODUS Super Series Integrity Training — User Guide

## Summary

Step-by-step guide for completing the mandatory Sportradar/MODUS Integrity Training (anti-match-fixing certification). Required before participation in MODUS Super Series. Training is delivered via web-based platform; completion is verified and recorded by MODUS admin.

**Training URL:** https://education.sportradar.com/MODUSOnlineIntegrityEducation/#/home

**Training provider:** Sportradar (UKDA-aligned, covers World Anti-Doping Code 2021).

## Digital location

`PKM/Documents/modus-super-series-integrity-training-user-guide.pdf` (source: Team Inbox, requires manual copy)

## Key steps (from guide)

1. **Register** on Sportradar education platform with name, email, password (min. 10 chars, uppercase + lowercase + number + symbol, no #)
2. **Activate email** — click activation link emailed immediately (critical step; without activation, no access)
3. **Save profile** with personal details (auto-filled after activation)
4. **Continue** to training content
5. **Watch video** (full length, no skipping; anti-cheating flag)
6. **Complete 10 multiple-choice questions** at end of video
7. **Receive completion certificate** — recorded in MODUS admin system

## Notes

- Non-completion results in tournament ineligibility
- Training focuses on anti-match-fixing, player conduct, and integrity in professional darts
- Certificate valid for the duration of Series participation (typically 1 year)
- Sander completed this training in November 2025 (see [[2025-11-21-modus-integrity-training-certificaat]])
