# MODUS Super Series — Tournament Format & Rules

## Summary

Official rulebook for the MODUS Super Series (13-week live-streamed professional darts tournament). November 2024 edition. Covers group structures, match formats, tiebreak rules, attendance requirements, force majeure clauses, and dispute resolution.

## Digital location

`PKM/Documents/2024-11-01-modus-super-series-tournament-format.pdf` (source: Team Inbox, requires manual copy)

## Key sections

- **Group allocation & play schedule:** Mon–Wed mornings (Group A), Thu–Fri evenings (Group B), Thu–Fri mornings (Group C)
- **Match format:** Best of 7 legs per match; 5 matches per player per session
- **Tiebreak rules:** Leg difference, overall legs won, head-to-head wins, average
- **Attendance requirement:** Players must arrive 90 min before session start, register with Players Marshal
- **Venue compliance:** 19 London Road, North End, Portsmouth, Hampshire PO2 0BQ
- **Force majeure:** Technical malfunction, epidemic, strike action, legal enactment — match can be postponed or abandoned
- **Finals structure:** Top 6 players qualify; split into two 3-player groups, best 4 to semi-finals, winners play for title
