# MODUS Online Limited

## What they do

MODUS Online Limited (MOL) operates the MODUS Super Series — a live-streamed professional darts tournament held in Portsmouth, UK. The series runs 13-week long league competitions with weekly finals and a grand final (13-week grand final worth £25,000 total prize pool).

**Venue:** MODUS Live Lounge, 19 London Road, North End, Portsmouth, Hampshire PO2 0BQ, UK.
